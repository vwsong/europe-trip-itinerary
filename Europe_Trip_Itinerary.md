# Europe Trip Itinerary
*April 11 - 21, 2025*

---

## TLDR: Book three things

1. SEA → LHR, BCN → SEA multi-city on Google Flights
2. London → Marrakesh on Ryanair
3. Marrakesh → BCN via British Airways Avios (transfer C1 points)

Note: To get to Marrakesh, there's a layover in London that gives you ~6.5h to get from Heathrow to Stansted airport.
